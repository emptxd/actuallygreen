Why This Matters

I picked climate change because its impacts are already hitting home across Europe, from record heatwaves to flooding, and we are running out of time to meet our targets. This project brings together emissions figures and policy commitments in a clear dashboard so that students, researchers and decision makers can see exactly where we stand and why urgent action is essential.
